How to Play STEAM Quiz
Game Rules:
  1. Think of any STEAM personality from the available characters list
  2. Answer the questions with Yes or No
  3. The game will try to guess your character in the fewest steps possible
  4. You can see your progress in the Steps counter
  5. Use the Characters button to see all available personalities
